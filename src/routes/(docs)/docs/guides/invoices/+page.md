# Invoices

## Viewing Invoices

The **Invoices** page lists all your invoices. Use the search bar to find invoices by number, client name, or status. You can filter and sort the list as needed.

## Creating an Invoice

1. Click **New Invoice**
2. Select a client from the dropdown (or create one first)
3. Add line items — type to search your catalog, or enter custom items
4. Each line item has a description, quantity, unit price, and calculated total
5. Review the invoice total and click **Save**

An invoice number is generated automatically based on your configured format.

## Editing an Invoice

Open an invoice and click **Edit**. You can modify all fields including line items, client, and status.

## Invoice Status

Invoices have the following statuses:

- **Draft** — Not yet sent to the client
- **Sent** — Delivered to the client
- **Paid** — Payment received
- **Overdue** — Past the due date without payment

## PDF Preview & Download

From the invoice detail page, you can preview the PDF rendering and download it. The PDF includes your business profile information, logo, and a formatted line item table.

## Currency

Each invoice has a currency field. The default is taken from your business profile, but you can change it per invoice when creating or editing. Amounts are formatted using the selected currency's symbol and decimal rules.

## Payer / Bill-To

Invoices can include a **Payer** (Bill-To) party separate from the client. When you select a client that has a linked payer, the payer fields are populated automatically. You can override the payer name, email, phone, address, and custom fields on each invoice. The payer details are captured as a snapshot when the invoice is saved.

## Bulk Actions

Select multiple invoices from the list to perform bulk operations like deletion.

## Change History

Every create, edit, and status change is recorded in the audit log. Click **Change History** on the invoice detail page to see a timestamped list of all modifications with field-level diffs.
