# Getting Started

Invoice Manager is a Progressive Web App that runs entirely in your browser. There is no account to create and no server to connect to — your data stays on your device.

## Access the App

Visit [dknathalage.github.io/invoices](https://dknathalage.github.io/invoices/) in any modern browser.

## Install as an App

Invoice Manager can be installed on your device for a native-like experience:

- **Desktop (Chrome/Edge):** Click the install icon in the address bar
- **iOS Safari:** Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
- **Android Chrome:** Tap the "Add to Home Screen" banner or use the browser menu

Once installed, the app launches in its own window and works offline.

## First Steps

1. **Set up your business profile** — Go to **Settings** and fill in your business name, address, and logo. This information appears on generated PDF invoices.

2. **Add a client** — Navigate to **Clients → New** and enter your first client's details.

3. **Create an invoice** — Go to **Invoices → New**, select a client, add line items, and save. You can preview and download the invoice as a PDF.

## Browser Support

Invoice Manager works in all modern browsers. It uses IndexedDB for data storage, which is supported in:

- Chrome / Edge 79+
- Firefox 78+
- Safari 14+
- All Chromium-based browsers

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. This means:

- Your data never leaves your device
- Clearing browser data will delete your invoices
- Use the **Export** feature in Settings to back up your data
